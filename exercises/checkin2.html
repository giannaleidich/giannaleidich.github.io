gianna's perfect breakfast recipe

Learn how to make an easy breakfast, perfect for busy college students who still want to eat healthy and consistently. This breakfast consists of delicious fried eggs and slices of bacon. You can read about the benefits of eating a healthy breakfast in this article by WebMD. This recipe also includes how to make your own iced coffee. You can read how much money you can save if you make your iced coffee at home, as opposed to buying it when you are out in this article by Security Banks.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 coffee of your choice
  • 1 pad of butter
  • Milk
  • Creamer

Instructions

Cooking the Eggs

  1. Turn stovetop on medium heat.
  2. Put the butter in a frying pan and wait until it melts completely.
  3. Crack the first egg and drop it in the pan.
  4. Crack the second egg and drop it in the pan, next to the first egg.
  5. Flip eggs after top has mostly solidified, usually takes 5 minutes.
  6. Flip eggs again after about another 5 minutes, or if you think they look ready.
  7. Place each egg on plate.

Cooking the Bacon

  1. Turn stovetop on medium heat.
  2. Place bacon in pan.
  3. Flip bacon after both have begun sizzling, usually takes 5 minutes.
  4. Flip bacon again after another 5 minutes. If you like extra crispy bacon, you can wait a bit longer.
  5. Place the bacon the plate.

Making the Iced Coffee

  1. Make coffee of your choice.
  2. Place coffee in the freezer for about a half hour to get chilled.
  3. Take coffee out of freezer.
  4. Add desired amount of milk and creamer.
