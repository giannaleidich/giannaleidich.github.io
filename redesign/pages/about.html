About the Designer

Gianna Leidich is a junior at the University of Massachusetts-Amherst studying English and computer science, and completing the Professional Writing and Technical Communications certificate.

A New York native, Gianna decided to redesign the official website of Westchester County, her home county, as an exercise in applying the best practices for digital accessibility to web design and development.

She created this website through an iterative design process that included usability testing, wireframing and prototyping, and creating mock-ups, drafts, and at last, the final website.

You can reach Gianna at her email or connect with her on LinkedIn.
