County Transportation

The Bee-Line Bus System

The Bee-Lee is Westchester County’s bus system which serves over 27 million passengers annually with convenient service connecting residents to jobs, school, recreation, shopping, and other regional transportation services

To reach the Bee-Line system, dial (914) 813–7777 weekdays between the hours of 8 A.M. and 8 P.M. and weekends between the hours of 8 A.M. and 4 P.M.

Westchester County Airport

The Westchester County Airport is one of the most active business aviation facilities in the nation. Total aircraft operations comprise approximately 20% commercial, 46% corporate, and 34% light general aviation.

Whether you are traveling out of the area for vacation of business, the airline service provides direct and connecting lights to a wide variety of domestic as well as international destinations.
