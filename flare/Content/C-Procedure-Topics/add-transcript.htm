Add a Transcript to a Video on SharePoint

You can generations transcripts for your video on SharePoint to promote accessibility and help all users access and navigate through your video. This module assumes you already have a video on SharePoint for which you want to create a transcript.

  1. Open your desired video on SharePoint.

  2. Navigate to the box next to Share on the Navigation Bar. A drop-down menu opens.

  3. Select Editing from the drop-down menu.

  4. Select Video settings from the right sidebar. A menu opens to the left of the sidebar.

  5. Select Transcript and captions. A drop-down menu opens.

  6. Click Generate. A prompt displays.

  7. Select your desired spoken language and click Generate. You may need to wait a few minutes as the transcript is automatically generated.

  8. Reload the page after the transcript has finished generating. Notice that there is a new Transcript section on the sidebar.

  9. Click Transcript on the sidebar. Your transcript generates.

  10. Click on any text box to edit its contents.
